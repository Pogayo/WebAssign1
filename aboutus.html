What do we do

African leadership University Market is ALU's number 1 market. For anything that you need, be it beverages, places to go or what to wear, we've got you covered. We partner with the best in Kigali to ensure the members of the community get what they deserve. Our references and partners are highly reliable, profesional and really nice people. If you are looking for anything around Kigali, this is the right place to be!

Our History

The African Leadership University Market was founded by an amazing team of two students.Being international, they found it difficult to find theings around Kigali. They could get very bad quality or even get exploited by the locals. In a bid to solve this problems for the ever-growing body of international students at ALU and even other expatriates in Kigali, they so it fit to start an online market where you could find literally anything. And here we are!
